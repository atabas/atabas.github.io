Hello, I am Anika

I am a software engineer, machine learning practitioner and writer.

I am currently a software developer at OpenText. Previously, I was a Frontend Engineer at Rubikloud (acquired by Kinaxis) and a Ruby on Rails Developer at FlipGive. I like to work on a variety of challenging problems in different domains and am always interested in learning, honing my craft and reinventing myself.

In terms of formal education, I have a Master's degree in Data Science and Analytics from Ryerson University and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from McGill University.

Whether it's tech, non-fiction, fiction or English literature, I am an avid reader. I believe there is enormous untapped potential in reading and writing, in being able to peek into another person's brain and creativity through words, in assimilating and disseminating knowledge effectively.
